Why Fellowship?

when we fellowship with others, it create space for members to pray for and support each other's needs.

House fellowship centers can serve as a platform for outreach and evangelism within local communities. Participants can share their joy's, burdens, and intercede for one another, building a culture of care, empathy, and compassion within the group. It also develop leadership skills as Participants can tke responsibilities as leading discussions, organzing activities or hosting gatherins. It als givs room for peopl with busy schedule to engage in regular fellowhip and worship.
