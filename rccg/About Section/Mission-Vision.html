The Redeemed Christain Church Of God Mission & Vision

  1. To accomplish No. 2 and 3 LLove, we will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries and within five minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries.
  2. We will pursue these objectives until every Nation in the world is reached for the Lord Jesus Christ.
